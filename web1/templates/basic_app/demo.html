DEMONSTRATION            




In this section we will walk you through a brief demonstration
of the PageRank Algorithm.





You might be wondering what is this image above?
There are 5 nodes, and every node is pointing to some other nodes.
Well, this is a nothing but a network of 5 webpages, where each node
is a webpage and every webpage has an outlink to another WebPage.

But how do we know which webpage is the most important?
How do we rank these pages?
The solution of this question is given by the PageRank Algorithm.
Click below to see how a surfer goes from a webpage to webpage and to understand that every outlink has specific weight.
You will get redirected to Page "0" by clicking the link here.
Click for Page "0"

Did you notice how we assigned weights to every links?
These weights can be seen as a measure for ranking of these webpages.
So we may say the page that has more incoming links with high weight may be the most important
