PAGERANK ALGORITHM      


Ever wondered how the search results are ranked?


The logic and maths behind it is given by the PageRank Algorithm



The PageRank Algorithm was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, the founders of Google, as part of their research at Stanford University.
Google used this algorithm for ranking the webpages till 2019. PageRank Algorithm is known to be very efficient and can be
applied at many places for ranking and optimisation.


In this project, we have reviewed and studied the PageRank Algorithm and implied the algorithm to solve a
real world problem-Airplane Route Optimization. Scroll Up and Tap the icon in the top left corner to navigate through
the Project Report, Project Presentation and a brief demonstration of the algorithm.




We have created this websie with HTML,CSS,JavaScript and Bootstrap in Frontend and Django in Backend.
